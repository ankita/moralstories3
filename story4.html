4..The Foolish Dog A dog was very hungry .He walked here and there looking for food.At last he got a piece of bone.He kept the bone in his mouth .He looked for a safe place to eat the bone.On the way he had to cross a small bridge .The dog looked down from the bridge .He saw his image in the water.He barked at the dog "bow wow ,bow "The bone in this mouth fell into the water .The dog felt ashamed .He ran away

Moral of the story:It is very foolish to be greedy.
